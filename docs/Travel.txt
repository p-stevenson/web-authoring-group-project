Travel Tips
Arriving
* Arrive at the airport early — it saves nerves and gives you time for everything you need.
* Before leaving home, always check your flight status online: make sure it hasn't been canceled or rescheduled.
* Prepare your documents and luggage in advance to avoid last-minute rush.
* Find out what services the airport offers: cafes, lounges, charging stations.
* Plan extra time for security and passport control — it can take longer than you expect.
Pack Light
* Keep important documents, chargers, medications, and other battery-powered electronics only in your carry-on.
* Pack efficiently so you can easily access your essentials.
* Check airline weight and size limits for luggage.
Check Documents
* Make sure you have your passport, tickets, and visas (if required).
* Check the expiration dates — especially for your passport and visas.
* Keep your documents accessible for easy presentation at checkpoints.
* Take photos or save digital copies of your documents in case they get lost.
